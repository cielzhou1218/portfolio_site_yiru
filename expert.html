The Problem

People who are starting career in the creative industry need a credible platform where they have options to connect with experts through video calls to discuss more in-depth topics, as well as ask quick questions and have them answered in a timely fashion. Because they want more flexibility in the ways they get expert advice.

The Concept

A progressive web app that allows users to schedule 1:1 online chats with their chosen creative-industry experts, as well as post questions to a community of like-minded individuals.

Why a progressive web app?

It is essential to have access to the app on both desktop and mobile, as video chat experience may be better on desktop, and quick, on-the-go actions can be taken on mobile device.

Why 1:1 online chat?

Users may need in-depth conversations with an expert of their choice, some questions cannot be answered in written form due to difficulty in communication, and 1:1 chat can help eliminate mis-understandings and provide clarity.

Why online forums?

Online forums allows users to post quick questions such as technical and certain consensus that can be easily answered in a timely manner, so that the users don't have to schedule a paid service every time when they need help from others in the field.
